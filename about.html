Hello! I am Michael Carlson, My pronouns are he/him and I am currently a student at Central Michigan University and studying graphic design.


Born in Wisconsin, and having lived all over the United States, I have always held an interest in different forms of art and had a lot of respect for artists, and I wanted to be able to enter the art world but I've never been the strongest draftsmen, so after experimenting with several different paths in life, I found out a passion I have for graphic design and many of the more technical aspects of art that aren't just drawing. After finding my passion for Graphic Design though, I have developed skills in areas that I was previously weak in.
