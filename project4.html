Circle Boogie

Animation is something I am passionate about so when I had the opportunity to take an animation class, I took it, and it was a class that opened my eyes and really challenged me and helped me gain confidence in the art that I made. This specific piece was an animation where we had to morph a circle into several different things and then back, and I chose villains from Role Playing games, specifically the Paper Mario, Final Fantasy, Dragon Quest, Kingdom Hearts and Shin Megami Tensei series.
